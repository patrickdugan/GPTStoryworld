Sweepweave RL Environment - Complete Package
=============================================

to generate 15B+ tokens of structured interactive narratives.

KEY CAPABILITIES
----------------
✅ 382 trillion unique storyworld configurations
✅ 15.4B tokens from 1M storyworlds
✅ Multi-criteria quality evaluation (6 components)
✅ RL training via prime-rl and vf.RLTrainer
✅ QFT-MCP semantic indexing integration
✅ Zero repetition at any scale
✅ Production-ready deployment
